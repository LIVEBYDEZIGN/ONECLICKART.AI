# ONECLICKART.AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-07-08

### 1. Information Collection and Use
ONECLICKART.AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable user data. All configuration settings (such as API keys and generation preferences) are stored strictly on your local browser storage (chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync).

### 2. Third-Party Transmission
When you choose to click the "2:3" or "3:2" buttons on an image overlay, the extension uses the image URL/base64 data and the webpage title to pass it to your Mama Banana app (at your specified localhost or hosted Netlify domain). This is done solely to facilitate AI image variation generation. No data is stored, collected, or logged by the extension author.

### 3. Permissions
The extension requests the following permissions to function:
- storage: To save settings locally.
- tabs: To check if the Mama Banana tab is already open.
- downloads: To download images directly.
- scripting & activeTab: To show error messages/alerts on the active tab.
- contextMenus: To register right-click options on images.
- sidePanel: To display the settings UI.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage users to check this page periodically for changes.
